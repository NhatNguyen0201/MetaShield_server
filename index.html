MetaShield: Metadata Eraser

Protect your digital privacy before you share.

100% Serverless & Offline

MetaShield is a lightweight, secure Android utility designed to inspect, strip, or modify hidden metadata (EXIF, GPS location, device info, and author names) from your photos, videos, and documents. Because it runs entirely on your device, your data never leaves your hands.

Core Features:

  • Deep Metadata Inspector: View hidden camera details, GPS coordinates, and timestamps.
  • One-Tap Sanitizer: Completely wipe metadata to create a clean, safe copy instantly.
  • Quick Share Sheet Integration: Clean files on-the-fly straight from Android's system share menu.
  • Premium Metadata Spoofing: Modify details or spoof GPS locations to maintain granular control over your privacy.
